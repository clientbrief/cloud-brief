## 1. Client Background

The client is a venture-backed startup preparing for a high-profile product launch.

Engineering decisions so far have favored speed over long-term maintainability.

---

## 2. Business Context

The company has a fixed launch date aligned with a marketing campaign and investor commitments.

Failure to launch on time is considered a higher risk than technical debt.

---

## 3. Current State

- Cloud-native stack with minimal governance
- Heavy reliance on managed services
- Limited observability setup
- No formal disaster recovery plan

---

## 4. Constraints & Limitations

- Small team under high delivery pressure
- Budget flexibility exists, but scrutiny is increasing
- No tolerance for large architectural changes pre-launch

---

## 5. Known Risks

- Operational blind spots
- Cost inefficiencies emerging post-launch
- Scaling assumptions not yet validated

---

## Decision Context Summary

This is a **speed-first environment** where future cleanup is explicitly deferred.
