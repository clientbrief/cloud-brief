# Cloud Client Brief  
**Scenario ID:** CB-CLOUD-003  
**Industry:** Healthcare Services  
**Company Size:** ~95 employees  
**Geography:** Southeast Asia  
**Cloud Maturity:** Low  
**Timeline Sensitivity:** High  

---

## 1. Client Background

The client operates a regional healthcare services platform handling sensitive personal and medical data.

Technology adoption has lagged behind business growth, largely due to regulatory concerns and risk aversion.

---

## 2. Business Context

A recent audit highlighted gaps in availability, data protection, and access control.

While no penalties were issued, management has been instructed to demonstrate “reasonable improvements” within a short timeframe.

Budget approval is conditional and limited.

---

## 3. Current State

- Single-region deployment
- Minimal redundancy
- Manual user access provisioning
- Ad-hoc backup procedures
- Limited logging and audit trails

---

## 4. Technical Requirements (Known)

- Improve baseline availability
- Strengthen access control and auditability
- Ensure data is protected at rest and in transit
- Maintain regional data residency

---

## 5. Constraints & Limitations

### Compliance
- Subject to healthcare data regulations
- Auditable controls expected, but certification not mandated

### Financial
- Budget approved only for compliance-related improvements
- No headcount increase allowed

### Organizational
- Small engineering team
- Compliance knowledge mostly external

---

## 6. Known Risks

- Over-scoping compliance initiatives
- Introducing controls the team cannot operate
- Misalignment between technical controls and audit expectations

---

## 7. Open Questions

- Required recovery objectives
- External audit frequency
- Long-term compliance roadmap

---

## Decision Context Summary

This scenario prioritizes **compliance visibility and defensibility**, not performance optimization.

---

## Disclaimer

This brief is fictional and does not represent a real healthcare provider.
