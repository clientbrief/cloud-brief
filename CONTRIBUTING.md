# Contributing to Client Brief

Client Brief welcomes contributions that align with its core philosophy of realism and constraint-driven decision making.

---

## Contribution Requirements

- All scenarios must be fictional
- No real company names or architectures
- Must follow BRIEF_STANDARD.md
- Focus on decision context, not solutions

---

## Review Criteria

Submissions are evaluated on:

- Realism of constraints
- Clarity of context
- Decision-making depth
- Professional tone

---

## Who Should Contribute

- Senior engineers
- Architects
- Engineering managers
- Professionals with real-world system experience

Beginner-level or tutorial-style content will not be accepted.
